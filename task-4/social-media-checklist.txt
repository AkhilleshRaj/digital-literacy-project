Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

DO’s:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful online presence.
2. Think before posting any content.
3. Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings.
4. Share useful and meaningful content.
5. Verify information before sharing it.

DON’Ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content.
3. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
4. Avoid accepting friend requests from unknown people.
5. Do not spread fake news or unverified information.
