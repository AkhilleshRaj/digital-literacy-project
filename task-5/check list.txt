Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Always verify the sender’s email address or phone number before clicking on any link.
2. Do not share passwords, OTPs, or personal details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a trusted organization.
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, SMS, or social media messages.
4. Check website URLs carefully; look for “https://” and avoid fake or misspelled domains.
5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts.
6. Keep your devices and software updated with the latest security patches.
7. Never approve unknown UPI payment requests; always check the recipient name before sending money.
8. Do not scan random QR codes, especially for receiving money, as it may lead to fraud.
9. Use secure and private Wi-Fi networks for financial transactions.
10. Immediately report any cybercrime at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in or call the helpline number 1930.
